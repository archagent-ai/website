Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Who We Are

ArchAgent ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at archagent.site and the platform at app.archagent.site. ArchAgent is a cloud platform for deploying and managing AI agents.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following data:

  • Account data: Name, email address, and authentication details when you sign up via Google OAuth.
  • API keys and tokens: Third-party API keys and bot tokens you provide for agent deployments. These are stored encrypted and used only to configure your agents.
  • Usage data: Agent deployment history, configuration choices, and platform interactions.
  • Website analytics: Pages visited, referral source, approximate location, device type, and browser. Collected via privacy-respecting analytics.
  • Payment data: Billing is handled by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers. Stripe's privacy policy governs payment data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the ArchAgent platform
  • To deploy, configure, and manage AI agents on your behalf
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To improve the platform and fix bugs
  • To communicate with you about your account or service changes

4. API Keys and Secrets

API keys and bot tokens you store in your ArchAgent profile are encrypted at rest and in transit. They are injected into your agent containers at deploy time and are not logged, displayed, or accessible to ArchAgent staff. You can delete your stored keys at any time from your profile.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google (Firebase): Authentication, database, and hosting.
  • Stripe: Payment processing.
  • GitHub Pages: Hosting for this marketing website.
  • Google Cloud Platform: Container infrastructure for agent deployments.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

6. Cookies

This marketing website (archagent.site) uses minimal cookies for basic analytics. The platform (app.archagent.site) uses cookies for authentication session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure. We use encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to production systems is restricted and audited. Agent containers are isolated from each other.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Agent deployment logs: Retained for 90 days after an agent is destroyed.
  • Payment records: Retained as required by law (typically 7 years).
  • Website analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 14 months.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the platform or email. Continued use of ArchAgent after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests, email privacy@archagent.site.

Effective date: 30 March 2026
