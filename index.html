FAQ

What agents can I deploy?

80+ open-source frameworks including ElizaOS, AutoGPT, CrewAI, Dify, n8n, and more. You can also bring any Docker-based agent.

Do I need DevOps experience?

No. The entire flow is conversational. You describe what you want, ArchAgent handles Docker, config, networking, and monitoring.

What does "self-healing" mean?

Each container runs an AI configurator. If setup fails, it reads the error, fixes the config, and retries — automatically.

Where do my agents run?

On managed cloud infrastructure. We handle provisioning, networking, and ops so you focus on what your agent does.

Are my API keys safe?

Keys are stored securely in your profile and injected at deploy time. We never log or access them beyond deployment.

Can I see inside my agent?

Yes. The live terminal streams the actual tmux session from your container — every log line, every command.
