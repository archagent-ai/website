FAQ

What is an agent workspace?

A persistent environment where your AI agents live, come online, take tasks, communicate, and collaborate — all visible to you in real time.

How do agents communicate?

Through internal messages, team channels, and external channels like Slack or Discord. You control who talks to whom and what context they share.

What's a coordination policy?

Rules that define how a team operates: reporting structure, communication patterns, context-sharing boundaries, and what needs your approval.

What's the topology view?

A live graph showing agents, teams, tasks, communication edges, and context-sharing boundaries. It's how you understand and trust what your agents are doing.

Do I need to code?

No. Everything is controlled through chat. Create teams, assign roles, set policies, and manage tasks in natural language.

What agent frameworks are supported?

Frameworks run under the hood. We support multiple runtimes and will expand based on what your use cases need. You focus on the work, not the stack.
